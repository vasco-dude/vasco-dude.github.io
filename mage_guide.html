Part I: The Foundations of the Arcane (Levels 1-29)

The Mage is Vesteria's oldest and most powerful magical faction. To begin your journey, reach Level 10 and speak with **Representative Jill** in Great Crossroads or seek out **Greybeard the Old** at the Mage's exclusive hub, the **Tree of Life**. Your power will primarily scale with **Intelligence (INT)**.

Core Skill Kit

  • Zap: Summons close-range lightning bolts that follow your cursor.
  • Mana Bomb: Fires a ball of mana that explodes on impact.
  • Thundercat: Calls down bolts of lightning from the sky at your cursor's location.
  • Blink: An instant teleport for mobility.

Core Mechanic: Stat-Based Skill Variants

A Mage's core skills can transform based on their stat allocation, creating unique hybrid builds. This requires at least 30 points in the specified stat.

Mana Bomb Variants:
  • Sonic Bomb (STR): A faster, lower cooldown, piercing projectile.
  • Multi Bomb (DEX): A spread-shot of several smaller bombs, perfect for applying on-hit effects like Venom Bomb.
  • Pure Bomb (INT): The standard version with a larger explosion.
Blink Variants:
  • Sonic Blink (STR): Becomes a damaging spell, hitting enemies you pass through. Upgrades to Supersonic Blink at 70 STR.
  • Holy Magic (Cleric): A subclass-specific variant that increases Blink's range by 50%.

Leveling Stat & Gear Progression (Levels 10-30)

Stat Priority:
Full Intelligence (INT)

Maximizes your early spell damage and provides a solid mana pool for all your abilities.

Key Gear to Acquire:
  • Weapon: Webbed Staff
  • Off-hand: The Enchiridion
  • Armor: Snowy Bandit Robes
  • Boots: Explorer's Yeti Boots

Part II: Choosing Your Calling (Level 30+)

At Level 30, your journey diverges. You must choose one of the five distinct specializations. This choice is permanent and will redefine your role on the battlefield. A build is a **pre-fight commitment**.

The Warlock - The Blood Reaper

A durable, dark magic user who sacrifices their own health to fuel powerful life-draining abilities and status effects. Their unique Blood Magic perk increases their damage in prolonged fights, rewarding a battle of attrition.

The Sorcerer - The Elemental Cataclysm

The Sorcerer is a "glass cannon" who wields destructive elemental spells. Their gameplay is a two-step process: prime a target with any spell to apply Lingering Magic, then detonate it with an elemental ability to trigger powerful bonus effects.

Sorcerer Build 1: The Relentless Sorcerer (Cooldown DPS)

Stat Allocation:

Key Synergy:
Uses the **Wise Beard** for a massive +40 INT. The **Ornate Staff**'s Divine Will perk adds constant damage, while **Capra's Robes** allows for controlled burst via Mana Detonation.

Sorcerer Build 2: The Wicked Sorcerer (Crit DPS)

The Cleric - The Divine Healer

The indispensable holy support class of Vesteria. Clerics use their abilities to heal allies, which in turn powers up their own magical damage thanks to the Holy Magic perk. **Resurrect** has a fixed 120s cooldown.

Cleric Build 1: The Cleric of Judgment (DPS)

Stat Allocation:
10 STR ‚Üí rest INT or 90 DEX (for Crit)
Focus on raw power or a crit-based build if you have the gear.
Key Synergy:
For raw power, use **Wise Beard** and **Ornate Staff**. For a crit build, assemble the **Wicked Witch Hat/Robes** and **"Keen"** prefix gear. The goal is to maximize damage while using healing for self-buffs.

The Songweaver - The Supportive Virtuoso

A pure support class that conducts the flow of battle with magical songs. Gameplay is a complex performance of building **Encore** stacks to empower your abilities, and **Reverb** stacks to control enemies.

[UPCOMING] The Shadowmancer - The Forbidden Master

Disclaimer: The Shadowmancer is an upcoming subclass. All information is based on developer showcases and is subject to change.

A master of forbidden arts with two distinct forms. The Mage-Form focuses on summoning Shade minions, while the Shade-Form transforms the caster into a hyper-aggressive melee assassin. This class uses Mana and a unique resource, **Shadow Points (SP)**.

Abilities Overview

Mage-Form (Summoner):
  • Extend Shadow: Inflicts a damage-reducing debuff.
  • Rend Shadow: Recharges your SP.
  • Subjugate: Revives slain enemies as temporary minions.
  • Summon Shade: Your core ability to summon up to 5 permanent minions.
  • Frenzy: Massively buffs your summoned Shades.
  • Shade Binding: Transform into Shade-Form.
Shade-Form (Melee Assassin):
  • Exalted Nightmare: Inflicts Fear on nearby enemies.
  • Exalted Leap: A forward-dashing attack.
  • Exalted Rampage: A frenzy that increases attack speed and provides lifesteal.
  • Exalted Shadow Assault: Teleport behind an enemy to apply debuffs.
  • Shade Claws (Passive): Alters your M1 attacks. Weapon perks are disabled.
  • Shade Release: Transform back to Mage-Form.
