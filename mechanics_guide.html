Primary Stats: The Foundation of Power

Your journey is defined by the choices you make, and none are more crucial than the allocation of your stat points. Upon each level up, you gain 3 points to invest in four Primary Stats. These stats not only provide direct bonuses but also unlock powerful Perks at key thresholds.

Secondary Stats: The Tangible Effects

These stats are derived from your Primary Stats, gear, and other effects. They represent your character's real-world effectiveness in combat and other activities.

Secondary Stat Overview

  • Attack (ATK): Your total current attack damage, combining level-based increases and your equipped weapon's stats.
  • Defense (DEF): Your total damage reduction, combining level-based increases and your equipped armor's stats.
  • Movement Speed (SPD) & Jump (JMP): Your character's mobility. Default values are 18 and 75, respectively.
  • Fortune (Greed / GRD): Increases the chance for double Mushtoken drops and affects fishing rarity.
  • Physical/Magical Attack Bonus (PYS/MAG): Percentage damage increases for Physical and Magical attacks, derived from STR and INT.
  • Agility (AGL): Your attack speed, derived primarily from DEX and gear.
  • Block Chance (BLK): The chance to block a physical attack, reducing damage by 50%. Influenced by DEX and gear.
  • Critical Strike Chance (CRT): The chance for a critical hit (default: +50% damage). Influenced by DEX and gear.
  • Warding: Your magical resistance. Functions as a "magic block," reducing incoming magic damage by a percentage.
