Core Mechanics

An In-Depth Analysis of Player Statistics

← Return to Main Codex

Welcome, Adventurer, to a foundational study of the very essence of your power. Understanding stats is the key to unlocking your full potential. Players are granted 3 stat points upon reaching each new level, allowing for deep customization. By level 10, you will have accumulated 27 points to begin shaping your legend.

Primary Stats

Strength (STR)

The embodiment of raw physical power, favored by Warriors and Hunters. Each point grants +0.075% increased physical damage and +0.5 flat physical damage.

  • 5 Points – Heavy Slash: "Double Slash" damage +5
  • 10 Points – First Strike: Damage to full health monsters +20%
  • 20 Points – Endurance: Stamina +1
  • 30 Points – Iron Will: Knockback Reduction +15%, Physical Defense +15
  • 50 Points – Hefty: +20 Physical Attack, +20 Physical Defense
  • 70 Points – Bastion: +30 Physical Defense
  • 90 Points – Swell: +30 Physical Attack
  • 120 Points – Tireless: Stamina depletion from abilities and sprinting reduced by 50%
  • 150 Points – Ruthless: All damage +10%

Dexterity (DEX)

A measure of agility, speed, and precision, essential for Hunters and Berserkers. Each point grants +0.45% attack speed and ~0.1667% critical strike chance.

  • 5 Points – Agility: Movement Speed +1%
  • 10 Points – Thievery: Monster Money Dropped +15%
  • 20 Points – Surefootedness: Movement Speed +1%, Jump +2
  • 30 Points – Swift: Gain 5% attack speed
  • 50 Points – Fast Hands: Consumable usage speed +40%
  • 70 Points – Unwavering: Reduce ability cooldowns by 15%
  • 90 Points – Deadeye: Critical Strike damage +10%
  • 120 Points – Supersonic: Movement Speed +2%, Jump +3
  • 150 Points – Destrous: Increase your total attack speed by 15%

Intelligence (INT)

Governs your command of the arcane, empowering Mages, Paladins, and Tricksters. Each point grants +0.15% increased magical damage, +0.75 flat magical damage, and +1 maximum MP.

  • 5 Points – Bookworm: Max MP +15, Magic Attack +5
  • 10 Points – Disciplined: XP Gain +5%
  • 20 Points – Persistent: MP Regeneration +20%, Magic Attack +10
  • 30 Points – Charismatic: Reduce shop costs by 20%
  • 50 Points – Resourceful: HP/MP from consumables +30%
  • 70 Points – Reactive: Magic Defense +30
  • 90 Points – Calculated: Max MP +100, MP Regeneration +15%
  • 120 Points – Focused: Magic Attack +30
  • 150 Points – Astral: Ignore 25% of magical defense

Vitality (VIT)

The source of resilience and survivability, crucial for tanks like Knights and Warlocks. Provides Health, HP regeneration, and defensive perks.

  • 5 Points – Regeneration: HP Regeneration +25%
  • 10 Points – Rejuvenation: HP Regeneration +30%
  • 20 Points – Fortitude: +8 DEF
  • 30 Points – Vigor: Area-effect abilities gain +10% Radius
  • 50 Points – Hearty: Max HP +100, HP Regeneration +20%
  • 70 Points – Tenacious: Duration of all crowd control effects reduced by 25%
  • 90 Points – Hearty II: Max HP +150, HP Regeneration +20%
  • 120 Points – Adaptive: Reduce damage from repeated instances by 4%, up to 20%
  • 150 Points – Juggernaut: Max HP +10%

Secondary Stats

These stats are derived from your Primary Stats, gear, and other effects, representing your character's tangible power and utility.

  • Attack (ATK): Your total damage, combining level-based increases and weapon stats.
  • Defense (DEF): Your total damage reduction, combining level-based increases and armor stats.
  • Movement Speed (SPD): Your movement speed. Starts at a base of 18.
  • Jump (JMP): Your jump height. Starts at a base of 75.
  • Fortune (GRD): Increases the chance for double Mushtoken drops and affects fishing outcomes.
  • Physical Attack Bonus (PYS): A percentage bonus to physical damage, derived from STR.
  • Magical Attack Bonus (MAG): A percentage bonus to magical damage, derived from INT.
  • Agility (AGL): Your attack speed, derived primarily from DEX.
  • Block Chance (BLK): The chance to block a physical attack, reducing damage by 50%. Influenced by DEX and gear.
  • Critical Strike Chance (CRT): The chance to land a critical hit for 50% bonus damage. Influenced by DEX and gear.
  • Warding: Your magical resistance. Reduces incoming magic damage. Functions like a "magic block."
