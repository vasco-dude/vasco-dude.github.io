The Hunter's Path

An In-Depth Guide to the Masters of Bow, Dagger, and Song

Base Hunter Assassin Ranger Trickster Bard

Part I: The Foundations of the Hunt (Levels 1-29)

Upon reaching Level 10, you can speak to a Representative Shepherd to become a Hunter. The Hunter class is defined by its agility, versatility, and high damage potential. From the start, you will learn to use both daggers for close-quarters combat and bows for ranged encounters.

Core Skill Kit

  • Double Jump (Passive): Enhances your natural mobility.
  • Execute (Dagger Skill): A powerful melee finisher for low-health targets.
  • Barrage (Bow Skill): A spread-shot of three arrows, perfect for close-range bursts.
  • Shunpo (Mobility Skill): A forward dash that deals damage and auto-equips your dagger, acting as a perfect engagement tool.

Leveling Stat & Gear Progression (Levels 10-30)

Stat Priority:
Full Strength (STR)

Early power comes from boosting base Physical Damage. Key weapons like the Spider Fang Dagger have a poison perk, Caustic Wounds, that scales directly with STR.

Key Gear to Acquire:
  • Dagger: Spider Fang Dagger
  • Headgear: Ratking Bucket (Quest Reward)
  • Armor: Snowy Bandit Armor

Part II: Choosing Your Path (Level 30+)

At Level 30, your journey truly begins. You must choose one of four distinct subclasses. This choice is permanent and defines your role. Remember, a build is a pre-fight commitment; you cannot swap gear in combat.

The Assassin - Master of Burst Damage

The Assassin is a fragile but lethal melee fighter who excels at eliminating key targets in a breathtakingly short burst of damage. Your gameplay revolves around two core mechanics: Assassin's Intent, which echoes a portion of your damage in a 2.5s window, and the Ravenous buff from Shadow Walk, which amplifies your damage against wounded targets.

Assassin Build 1: The Burst Assassin

Stat Allocation:
150 STR → 150 DEX → rest STR
This balances raw power with the necessary speed and crit to be effective.
Key Synergy:
Stack Critical Damage from The Incisor (+25%) and Vampiric Vest (+25%) for a massive +50% total. Use the **Abyssal Dagger**'s Echo perk to triple your Execute hits within the burst window.

Assassin Build 2: The Cryo Assassin

Stat Allocation:
Full Dexterity (DEX)
Your only goal is applying stacks, and DEX provides the highest Attack Speed.
Key Synergy:
This build requires the **Frozen Forgotten Dagger**. Its Cryo Cascades perk deals damage equal to **10% of the enemy's maximum HP** after enough hits. Use the **Shelmet** for more damage per hit or the **Mogloko Mask** for faster stacking.

The Ranger - Master of Archery

The Ranger is Vesteria's most versatile damage dealer, using a single skill, Stance, which completely transforms their arrows and playstyle based on their highest stat.

Ranger Build 1: The Dex Ranger (Sustained DPS)

Stat Allocation:
Half DEX, Half STR (1 more DEX)
A hybrid build to power up your arrows (STR) while enabling the rapid-fire DEX Ranger's Stance.
Key Synergy:
Focus on critical hits. Stack +50% Crit Damage from The Incisor and **Vampiric Vest**. Use the **Abyssal Bow** for long-range damage or the **Forgotten Bow** to execute low-health bosses.

Ranger Build 2: The Str Ranger (Sniper)

Stat Allocation:
Full Strength (STR)
Maximizes the power of each devastating shot from the STR Sniper's Stance.
Key Synergy:
Combine the **Straggly Beard** (+40 STR) with the **Abyssal Bow**. The extreme range of your stance amplifies your damage via the bow's perk. Stack Crit Damage with **The Incisor** and **Vampiric Vest** to make every critical shot a massive event.

Ranger Build 3: The Int Ranger (Explosive Mage)

Stat Allocation:
Half INT, Half DEX (1 more INT)
Enables the INT Spellslinger's Stance while providing the attack speed to fire more explosions.
Key Synergy:
Your explosions can crit! Use **Ricochet** to bounce your exploding arrows. Stack +50% Crit Damage with **The Incisor** and **Vampiric Vest** to make your AoE devastating. Choose between the **Wise Beard** (+40 INT) or **Mustache** (+40 DEX) to enable your stat spread.

The Trickster - Master of Traps & Tactics

The Trickster is a dagger-wielding fighter who controls the battlefield through cunning debuffs, traps, and unique magical abilities. They are known as counters to "glass cannon" builds.

Trickster Build 1: The "Stat" Stick Trickster (DoT & Control)

Stat Allocation:
70 DEX → rest INT
Focuses on powering up the magical effects and debuffs granted by your gear.
Key Synergy:
Requires the **Jade Bow** for its massive AoE slow. Use the **Jade Dagger** to apply a true damage debuff to bosses, or the **Starshroom Dagger** to turn your Shunpo into an AoE DoT field. The **Dragon Grunt Headband** modifies Bubble Burst for easier debuff application.

Trickster Build 2: The Str Trickster (Hit-and-Run)

Stat Allocation:
90 DEX → rest STR
A brawler build that focuses on landing a flurry of powerful M1 attacks and escaping safely.
Key Synergy:
Use the **Jade Bow** to slow enemies, then attack with your dagger. Your escape is key: The **Mythic Mushroom Boots** reduce jump stamina cost, allowing you to spam your maxed-out **Disengage** skill. The **Gravedigger Mask** adds another dash via Execute for unparalleled mobility.

The Bard - Master of Support

The Bard is Vesteria's premier support class, a selfless anchor who uses magical songs played on unique instruments to empower allies, control enemies, and ensure victory.

Bard Build 1: The Int Bard (Caster Support)

Stat Allocation:
70 DEX → rest INT
The quintessential caster, focusing on spell damage with Crescendo and high mana for sustained buffing.
Key Synergy:
The **Moko Drum** is the heart of your damage, doubling the power of your fourth Crescendo. Equip **Snow Goggles** to gain a new damage spell, Chilling Gale. Use the **Jade Bow** for crowd control.

Bard Build 2: The Str Bard (Battle Skald)

Stat Allocation:
70 DEX → rest STR
A front-line fighter who buffs allies while dealing significant physical damage.
Key Synergy:
This build requires the **Grizzly Head** and **Grizzly Armor**. Together, they grant the Piercing Roar ability, a powerful STR-scaling AoE attack unique to this setup. The +35 STR from the helmet is your main source of power.

Bard Build 3: The Vit Bard (The Unkillable Anchor)

Stat Allocation:
70 STR → 70 DEX → rest VIT
A selfless support focused on extreme survivability to guarantee your buffs never falter.
Key Synergy:
Your tankiness comes from two items: the **Gravedigger Vest**, whose Death's Door perk turns burst damage into a manageable DoT, and the **Tribute Lyre**, which provides massive magic defense and a 10% block chance. You will outlive everything.
