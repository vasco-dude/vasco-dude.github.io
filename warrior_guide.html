Part I: The Foundations of the Warrior (Levels 1-29)

Upon reaching Level 10, swearing allegiance to the Warriors at the Redwood Stronghold grants you a **significant 50% maximum HP boost**, defining you as a front-line combatant from the start. Unlike other classes, a base Warrior **cannot use an off-hand item**. Your Warrior Rune is a utility item that teleports you to the stronghold.

Core Skill Kit

  • Triple Slash (Passive): Your primary three-hit M1 combo.
  • Spin Slash (Active): A classic AoE spin attack.
  • Startling Slash (Active): A magical ground-blast, giving early access to magic damage.
  • Combat Roll (Active): A defensive roll providing a massive 65% damage reduction.
  • Parry (Active): A high-skill block that negates damage and delivers a counter-attack.
  • Ground Slam (Active): A high-damage AoE leap slam.

Leveling Stat & Gear Progression (Levels 10-30)

Stat Priority:
Full Strength (STR)

Maximizes your physical damage, setting a strong foundation for any future subclass.

Key Gear to Acquire:
  • Weapon: Coral Club
  • Headgear: Ratking Bucket
  • Armor: Snowy Bandit Armor

Part II: Choosing Your Vow (Level 30+)

At Level 30, you will specialize into one of three distinct subclasses. This permanent choice will redefine your role on the battlefield. A build is a **pre-fight commitment**.

The Paladin - The Holy Hybrid

The Paladin is a holy warrior who blends martial prowess with divine magic, able to deal damage, heal allies, and control the battlefield with sacred power.

Paladin Build 1: The Int Paladin (Glass Cannon Spell-Slinger)

Stat Allocation:
10-30 STR ‚Üí rest INT
Minimal STR for gear requirements, all other points into INT to maximize spell damage.
Key Synergy:
A high-risk build using **Gladiator Armor** for its Reckless perk (+20% damage dealt & received). Use the **Snel Helm** for max INT, and the **Pumpkin Brute Boots** to turn your Combat Roll into another offensive spell.

Paladin Build 2: The Str Paladin (The Overwhelming Crusader)

Stat Allocation:
Full Strength (STR)
Focuses on maximizing the physical damage of every greatweapon swing.
Key Synergy:
The **Ghostflame Greataxe** is the centerpiece. Its Tormenting Consecrate perk creates a damage-amplification field, making your powerful STR-based attacks hit even harder. The **First Mate's Anchor** is an alternative for the highest raw physical damage in the game.

The Berserker - The Furious Damage-Dealer

The Berserker is the ultimate glass cannon, forgoing all defense for the highest sustained and AoE damage in the game. Its core mechanic, the Unrelenting perk, provides damage and lifesteal that changes dynamically based on your current HP.

Berserker Build 1: The M1 Berserker (Unrelenting Assailant)

Stat Allocation:
150 STR ‚Üí 150 DEX ‚Üí rest STR
Hits crucial STR and DEX perks for a blend of high damage and extreme attack speed.
Key Synergy:
Weapon synergy is key. Combine the **Cholla Blade** and **Decorated Antler** to turn your Triple Slash into an AoE event. Use **Azariah's Edge** for stacking attack speed on crits.

Berserker Build 2: The Ability Berserker (Whirlwind of Steel)

Stat Allocation:
90 or 150 DEX ‚Üí rest STR
DEX is for unlocking damage perks; your main damage comes from STR-scaling abilities.
Key Synergy:
Requires the **Jade Blade**. Its Jade Blast perk transforms your Startling Slash into a devastating multi-hit STR nuke. This is best paired with the **Gladiator Armor** for a +20% boost to your entire ability rotation.

Berserker Build 3: The Hybrid Berserker (The Perfect Storm)

Stat Allocation:
90 DEX ‚Üí rest STR
An endgame aspiration that relies on achieving high Crit Chance from rare Cursed Scroll upgrades on weapons.
Key Synergy:
Combine crit-scrolled weapons like **Azariah's Edge** and **Jade Blade**. Use the **Gladiator Armor**, as its +20% damage bonus applies to both your M1s and abilities, creating a storm of damage with no downtime.

Berserker Build 4: The Cryo Berserker (AoE Stacking Specialist)

Stat Allocation:
Full Dexterity (DEX)
Damage comes from the 10% max HP burst of Cryo Cascades; DEX provides the Attack Speed to apply stacks faster.
Key Synergy:
Requires a Cryo weapon (**Frozen Moko Club** or **Frozen Mushroom Sword**). Use the **Gladiator Armor**'s +20% damage bonus to amplify the final Cryo burst. The Berserker's cleaving M1 attacks are perfect for applying stacks to large groups.

The Knight - The Unbreakable Protector

The Knight is the quintessential tank, defined by their unique ability to wield a shield and their Knight's Might perk, which increases ability damage based on their bonus HP.

Knight Build 1: The Block/Warding Knight (Impenetrable Fortress)

Stat Allocation:
90 STR ‚Üí rest VIT
90 STR unlocks the Bustle damage perk; VIT provides maximum survivability and fuels your damage.
Key Synergy:
A build focused on Block Chance. The core is the three-piece combo of **Urchin Helmet**, **Tuaa Shield**, and **Hobo Armor** for a massive +45% Block Chance. The **Fallen Blade** is the ideal weapon, as its damage scales with your high VIT stat.

Knight Build 2: The Stat Knight (The Balanced Juggernaut)

Stat Allocation:
Half VIT, Half STR
A perfect 50/50 split creates an ultimate bruiser who is both extremely tanky and a major damage threat.
Key Synergy:
Turn defense into offense. The **Valkyrie Helmet** applies a permanent slow to melee attackers. The **Haunted Sigil of Malisis** builds stacks as you take damage, allowing you to unleash a massive burst. The **Soulburn Edge** turns Ground Slam into a persistent fire AoE.
